General Medical Services
at
Ade-Tade Hospital

We provide convenient care for all your medical concerns

Out-patient services

Many conditions can be treated quickly without an overnight stay. If your condition does not require admission, we will take care of you or your loved one and send you home with your medications and all necessary information, in no time.

In-patient services

If your condition requires that we monitor you closely or carry out procedures such as giving you fluids and drugs through your veins, we will admit you in our facility and our fantastic team of nurses will be with you 24 hours a day to take care of you in a clean, quiet and comfortable environment.

Preventive and screening program

We believe that prevention is better, cheaper and generally easier than cure.

Taking small steps to prevent hypertension, diabetes, heart problems, stroke, and other diseases can lead to a better quality of life. In cases where the condition has already occurred, early detection, treatment, and routine monitoring can make life more enjoyable.

Our preventive and screening services include:

  • Cardiovascular disease screening
  • Diabetes screening
  • Diabetes self-management training
  • Obesity screenings & counseling
  • Cervical & vaginal cancer screening
  • Colorectal cancer screening
  • Prostate cancer screening
  • Breast cancer screening (mammogram)
  • Hepatitis C screening test
  • HIV screening
