Our Mission

Welcome to Ade-Tade Hospital. Our mission is to provide high-quality and affordable care, with the best consideration for patient satisfaction. Our team is knowledgeable, compassionate, and focused on the treatment and prevention of illnesses, and the maintenance of your health in a comfortable and respectful environment.

Our Services

We provide a variety of services tailored to your healthcare needs. Health is wealth; that's why we are here to help you stay healthy so that you can enjoy the precious moments of life. Visit us for treatment of general medical and surgical illnesses, checkup, antenatal care and delivery, family planning, and chronic disease counseling. And for your urgent healthcare needs, we are open 24/7.
