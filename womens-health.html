Women's Health
at
Ade-Tade Hospital

We believe in the comprehensive care of all women, before, during, and after child bearing years.
We have specific sevices tailored to your needs at every stage.

Family Planning

To ensure health for every woman and her family, it is helpful to plan for every pregnancy and to adequately space child births.

We provide several family planning services including contraceptive services, counseling, and pregnancy testing in a compassionate and supportive environment.

We also offer fertility counseling and fertility awareness education.

Antenatal care and delivery

We provide personalized, one-on-one, skilled, and compassionate care for every pregnant woman and her baby.

When you register with us, we get to know you and understand your health needs. We monitor you and your baby as your baby grows and your body changes, educate you about what to expect, and answer your questions as you progress on this amazing journey.

Ultimately, together with you, we develop a plan for your delivery, and like family members, stand by your side as we help you bring your baby into the world.

We understand that having a new addition to the family can be overwhelming, so before sending you home, we make sure to address your concerns and prepare you and your family members, to help make the change easier for you.

To find out how to register with us, please call us at 0806-635-8086.

Screening services for women

Our screening program includes tests and monitoring for factors that can contribute to heart attack and stroke. These include blood cholesterol, blood pressure, blood sugar, and body weight.

For women in particular, our screening program also includes routine pap smears, and breast exams as appropriate for your age.

Learn more about our comprehensive preventive and screening program here.
